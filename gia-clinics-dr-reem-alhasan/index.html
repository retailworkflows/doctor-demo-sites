Dr. Reem Alhasan

General and Cosmetic Dentist at GIA Clinics

Dr. Reem Alhasan is a highly trained general and cosmetic dentist with more than 12 years of experience. She holds a Doctorate of Dental Surgery from Jordan University, completed a higher diploma in Implantology from Deutsche Gesellschaft, completed American Boards part one and two in New York, and added a laser mastership with the American Academy of Laser. Her work focuses on digital smile design, veneers, high-quality dental treatment, and comfortable patient care.
