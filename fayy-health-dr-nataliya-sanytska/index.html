Doctor profile

Dr. Nataliya Sanytska

Board-Certified Aesthetic Dermatologist at FAYY Health

Dr. Nataliya Sanytska is an experienced board-certified aesthetic dermatologist with over 12 years of experience across the Gulf and Europe. She is known as an international medical trainer and speaker, with a skin-first approach focused on tissue restoration, natural results, and avoiding overfilling. She earned her medical degree from the National Medical University of O.O. Bogomolec in Ukraine, completed a master's degree in Dermatology and Aesthetic Medicine at the Ukrainian National Medical Academy of Postgraduate Education, and refined her skills through further training in Germany and Spain.
