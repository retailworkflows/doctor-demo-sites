Doctor profile

Dr. Lara El Hayderi

Specialist Dermatologist at DermaMed Clinic

Dr. Lara El Hayderi graduated from Liege University in Belgium with a Medical Master in 2010, completed dermatology residency training at the University of Liege in 2014, and completed a PhD at the University of Liege in 2016 on infectious diseases. She worked for more than 10 years at the University Department of Liege as Clinical Chief, taught general and cosmetic dermatology, and has spoken at national and international congresses in Canada, the USA, Europe, and the UAE. Her clinical interests include skin cancer surgery, aesthetic dermatology, general dermatology, inflammatory and allergic skin disease, hair and nail disease, infectious disease, and mole checks.
